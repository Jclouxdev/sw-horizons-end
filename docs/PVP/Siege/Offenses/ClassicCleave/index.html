Dova can be replaced by Teon or Konamiya (Any monster who give instant turn to another monster).

Pretty basic Mihyang Dova/Teon/Konamiya Lushen.

The idea is Dova boost Lushen to full attack bar while giving him attack buff. Lushen then uses s3 and Mihyang follows up with s2 to finish anything off.

This offense is good into any non fire heavy offense and non speed lead offenses.
