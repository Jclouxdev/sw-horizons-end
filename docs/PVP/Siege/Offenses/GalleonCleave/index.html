Kaki can be replaced by any cleave unit.
Examples : Alicia , Pungbeak , Teshar , Julie...

ROBO-G92 can be replaced by Tiana for a far more reliable offense.

The idea is ROBO-G92 strips, Galleon def breaks and provides attack buff then your dmg dealer aoes for the win. There is a ton of damage dealer options depending on the offence you are facing.

This offense does require very tight speed tuning, you cant rely on robo to reduce attack bar so all units should be within a few speed of each other Good into many defenses but not 100% reliable (Galleon is usually banned from being used in higher ranked siege along with wind robo for this reason)
